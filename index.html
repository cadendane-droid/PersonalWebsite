about me

I'm Caden Ice — a Business Computer Information Systems student at the University of North Texas, headed toward product ownership and Agile leadership. Right now I'm building Almura, a tool that helps students turn scattered conversations into relationships they actually keep.

Underneath the product is a belief I keep circling back to: depth beats volume — in the people you know, the work you make, and the way you make it. the log
thoughts on growth

I've stopped trusting the version of growth that's really just accumulation — more books finished, more tickets closed, more moved through. The growth that's held up for me looks slower: returning to the same few ideas until they change how I act, not just what I can talk about.

The same instinct runs through the product I'm building and the way I learn a codebase or a car. Depth costs patience up front and pays it back quietly. I'd rather know a few things — and a few people — all the way down.

So this page will stay short, and grow slowly. That's the point of it.
